About us

Babycare was founded in 2018, founded with all my heart and ready to serve

Babycare is one of the training and placement institutions for Indonesian domestic workers and babysitters that covers the Jabodetabek area, implementing a No Salary Deduction System because a pay cut is an injustice for workers.

The workforce provided here is a trained and educated workforce to be able to help all household needs. With the aim of helping to ease your household tasks and helping government programs in alleviating unemployment. Underline comfort and satisfaction for workers and service users.

Services

some of the services we provide

Daytime childcare

When you need childcare cover during the day – we can help. Sitters provides experienced childcare professionals, straight to your door. For as little as three hours or for a whole day – even at short notice.

Evening babysitting

Let us make your social life simple. Book trusted, award-winning Sitters childcare online and we'll send you fully vetted childcare professionals, straight to your door.

Emergency childcare

As any parent knows, needing last-minute childcare can be stressful. So instead of making frantic phone calls to friends and family, Sitters can arrange childcare with as little as 30 minutes notice, wherever and whenever you need it.

Work demands

Juggling work and childcare can be challenging – even with the best-laid plans in place. If you find yourself needing back-up childcare, Sitters provides you with fast access to experienced childcare professionals, whenever you need them.

Hotels & on holiday

Sitters can also provide childcare when you're staying away from home. Whether you're in a hotel, holiday let or staying with family and you need childcare – we can help.

Weddings & events

If you have an important event to attend and need help with childcare (either at home or while you're there), we can help. Sitters can provide babysitters to look after your little ones, freeing you up to relax and enjoy the celebrations.
